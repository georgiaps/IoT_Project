Welcome to City Microclimate and Traffic Analysis

Our platform provides a comprehensive overview of microclimate conditions and traffic patterns in key locations around the city of Patras. Use the interactive map to explore real-time data from advanced IoT sensors on temperature, percipitacion, humidity and traffic congestion. With dynamic visualizations and timely alerts, we aim to enhance urban planning, improve traffic management, and contribute to a healthier, more sustainable city environment. Explore the data, monitor critical points, stay informed and make smarter decisions for your commute with our intuitive and innovative tools.

Wind Speed

Strong winds on the Rio-Antirrio Bridge pose a significant risk to both vehicles and micromobility users, such as cyclists and electric scooters. This alert not only informs users about dangerous wind conditions but also helps prevent potential accidents caused by vehicle instability or difficulty controlling micromobility devices. By staying alert to these conditions, we can reduce the chances of traffic disruptions and ensure safer travel for all bridge users, whether on the road or using alternative mobility options.

Temperature

Extreme heat can seriously affect road conditions, vehicle performance, and personal safety. High temperatures can lead to tire blowouts, engine malfunctions, and even road surface damage, posing risks to drivers, cyclists, and pedestrians alike. This alert serves to warn users about the dangers of traveling in such conditions, allowing them to make safer choices—whether it's delaying trips, staying hydrated, or using safer, cooler routes. By raising awareness of extreme temperatures, we protect the well-being of everyone on the road and in transit.

Rain Amount

When rainfall exceeds 3.5mm at the National Road Interchange, road conditions deteriorate rapidly, increasing the risk of skidding and reduced visibility—especially at the sharp and dangerous turn in this area. Wet pavement can make braking less effective, while pooled water can lead to aquaplaning, making it crucial for drivers to slow down and remain vigilant. This alert serves as an early warning, urging extra caution to prevent accidents in this high-risk zone.

Current Alerts

Excessive rainfall near Agyias Beach significantly increases the risk of flooding, making both the roadway and the adjacent bicycle path hazardous for vehicles, cyclists, and pedestrians. Water accumulation can reduce traction, cause hydroplaning, and create unpredictable conditions for micromobility users. This alert helps travelers stay informed and take necessary precautions, ensuring safer movement in this flood-prone coastal area, especially during heavy downpours.
